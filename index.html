Trust model

Biography first, AI second.

Lifaria treats privacy, consent, and ownership as core product features. The goal is not to automate your life story away from you. It is to help you find the shape, then keep you in charge of what becomes official.

Private by default

No source, moment, chapter, invite, or export becomes public without explicit action.

Source-backed interpretation

Suggestions show evidence signals like source clusters, dates, places, tags, and narrative clues.

User sovereignty

You accept, reject, rename, rewrite, and export. Lifaria proposes structure, then waits for you.

Manual mode

For sensitive work, restrict AI to organizing, sequencing, tagging, and deduplicating.
